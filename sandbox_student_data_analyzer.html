Critical Analysis Checklist

After reviewing the AI-generated recommendations, evaluate the output carefully before acting on it. Check off each item as you verify it.

Data Analysis Output Quality Checklist
Accuracy: The AI correctly interpreted my data — it did not misread the numbers or confuse performance bands
Relevance: The recommendations are appropriate for my grade level, subject area, and student population
Actionability: The suggestions are specific enough for me to actually implement — not just general advice
Equity awareness: The AI addressed the needs of all student subgroups, including ELL students, students with IEPs, and advanced learners
Missing context: I can identify at least one important thing about my students or classroom that the AI did not — and could not — know
Standards alignment: The recommended strategies connect logically to the skills identified as gaps in my data
